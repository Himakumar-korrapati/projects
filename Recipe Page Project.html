Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

A deliciously moist and sweet banana bread with rich chocolate chips. Perfect for breakfast or dessert!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mashed bananas and melted butter.
  3. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the baking soda and salt, then mix in the flour until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips, then pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
